Fun Facts

The Sassy Side Eye Master
Mickey is a master of the "side eye"! He has perfected that sassy, sideways glance that can melt hearts and make you burst out laughing at the same time.
The Adorably Observant Nap Master
Mickey's napping skills are top-notch, but don't be fooled by his tranquility. With one eye open, he expertly judges and observes your every move, all while maintaining an adorable and loving presence. It's like having your very own judgmental yet lovable furry companion!
The Smiling Joy Bringer
Get ready for a smile overload! Mickey has a contagious smile that can brighten up even the gloomiest of days. His happy expression is a constant reminder to find joy in the simplest things.
The Majestic Sitting King
When it comes to sitting, Mickey has mastered the art of regal elegance. With a posture fit for royalty, he sits like a true king, demanding the attention and admiration he rightfully deserves.
The Affectionate Love Language Expert
Attention is Mickey's love language! He craves and thrives on affection and will shower you with love and gratitude in return. Prepare for endless cuddles, playful antics, and unwavering loyalty as you become the center of his world.
